Our Services

AIR FREIGHT

PRIME provides an extensive range of air freight services. We serve with complete solution to your every airfreight demand, from documents to pallets. Through our worldwide network and proactive staffs, you can save your time, energy and enhance your business whilst experiencing the fastest, efficient and cost-effective import and export freight Services.

OCEAN FREIGHT

Provide us the details of the location of your goods and PRIME will ship it for you. Through our increasing networks and connections, we can give you access to worldwide carriers covering major ports, with best transit time.

WAREHOUSING

PRIME had developed and launched a unique and smart warehousing solution where you can access and monitor your stocks at your fingertips. Real time stocks inventory using the FIFO inventory system.

LAND FREIGHT

For your ultimate convenience, PRIME caters local collection and delivery services. We will pick-up and drop your cargo at your door steps.
We also offer land export to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia with high insurance at a competitive rate.
